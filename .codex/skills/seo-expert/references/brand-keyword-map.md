# Brand Keyword Map

Use this file when choosing page keywords, rewriting titles, or deciding the primary search intent of a page.

## Core Brand Terms

- Christian Guzman
- Engineering Leadership
- Engineering Leadership Coaching
- Human Systems Architecture
- Engineering Mentor
- Fractional CTO
- Technical Debt Consulting
- Coaching

## Page Intent Patterns

### Home

- Primary goal: establish who Christian Guzman is and what he helps with.
- Preferred keyword themes:
  - `Christian Guzman`
  - `Engineering Leadership Coaching`
  - `Fractional CTO`
  - `Human Systems Architecture`
- Title patterns:
  - `Christian Guzman | Engineering Leadership Coaching`
  - `Christian Guzman | Fractional CTO And Engineering Mentor`

### About Or Manifesto

- Primary goal: deepen authority, philosophy, and leadership point of view.
- Preferred keyword themes:
  - `Engineering Leadership`
  - `Human Systems Architecture`
  - `Technical Leadership Philosophy`
  - `Coaching`
- Keep tone thoughtful and credible. Do not overstuff service keywords if the page is mostly narrative.

### Services

- Primary goal: match explicit commercial intent.
- Preferred keyword themes:
  - `Engineering Leadership Coaching`
  - `Technical Debt Consulting`
  - `Fractional CTO`
  - `Engineering Mentor`
- Titles and descriptions should make the service outcome legible within the first sentence.

### Blog Posts

- Primary goal: target topic-specific intent while reinforcing the broader brand.
- Use one primary topic keyword plus one light brand-supporting keyword when natural.
- Good supporting brand terms:
  - `Engineering Leadership`
  - `Coaching`
  - `Human Systems Architecture`

## Keyword Selection Rules

- Pick one primary keyword intent per page.
- Add secondary keywords only when they genuinely fit the page.
- Prefer specificity over breadth.
- Avoid repeating the full service keyword set on every page.
- Preserve natural language and credibility over density.
