About Me

Build Resilient Organizations

My focus isn’t just on resilient code or perfect processes. I look at the underlying human dynamics to build strong, adaptable, and high-performing teams that can weather any storm.

Remove the Unseen Friction

A well-run engineering organization is a system, not just an asset. I create clarity of purpose and remove the “sludge” that prevents talented people from doing their best work, allowing them to excel.

Focus on Human Systems

The most expensive issues are often systemic and human, not technical. My philosophy is to understand the disconnect between what we say and how we actually behave to build lasting greatness.
